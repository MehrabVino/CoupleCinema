# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Assume good intent, ask for clarification before escalation.
- Accept and act on maintainers' technical review decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Deliberate disruption or bad-faith participation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting conduct issues

Report incidents privately to maintainers with:

- what happened
- when and where
- screenshots/logs if available

All reports are reviewed in good faith and handled as confidentially as possible.
